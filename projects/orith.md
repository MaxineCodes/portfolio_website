Orith, the Moth Mage. She uses fae magic from the realm of dreams to perplex her enemies.
This artwork is based on the concept artwork of Alexis Pflaum. Their work was really interesting to me, and I enjoyed remaking it in 3D! Link to the original concept artwork:

For my workflow, I started off in Zbrush, then moved onto Maya and finally textured in 3DCoat and Photoshop. Basic rigging and posing was also done in Maya.
